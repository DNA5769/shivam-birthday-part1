Shivam's Run™

I wanted to make a game about Shivam's entire day i.e him walking. However, I also didn't want to copy Death Stranding and hence I made a game about Shivam running and listening to music, where he will come across many items which may/may not help him. This game is also a test in how well you know Shivam as a person.
